Branches

Logic: The study of correct reasoning. What are the laws of logic? How can we derive valid conclusions? How do we distinguish between good and bad arguments?

Epistemology: The study of knowledge. What is truth? Can our beliefs be justified? How do we know what we know? Can we trust our senses and reasoning? How does the scientific method relate to other methods of acquiring knowledge? How do we investigate and interpret the past? Does history have a purpose?

Metaphyics: The study of the nature of ultimate reality. What is the nature of being, existence, causality, space, time, substance? Are there abstract entities? What is the relationship between mind and matter? What is consciousness? Do we have free will?

Value: The study of ethics and values. What is the nature of right and wrong? Is morality objective? What is the nature of art and beauty?
