Church History

Church history can be split into 3 periods: Ancient (AD 30-590), Medieval (590-1517), and Modern (1517-present).

However, these periods are very broad, so it helps to also have narrower periods to think about: Apostolic (AD 30-100), Persecuted (100-324), Nicene (325-590), Early Medieval (590-800), High Medieval (800-1294), Late Medieval (1294-1517), and Modern (1517-present).
