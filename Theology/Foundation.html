Theological Material and Ideas

Revelation

Scripture

The Bible is the only sufficient, certain, and infallible standard of all saving knowledge, faith, and obedience. The Lord was pleased at various times and in various manners to reveal himself and to declare his will to his church. Then, to better preserve and propagate the truth, and to better establish and comfort the church against the corruption of the flesh and the malice of Satan and the world, God put his revelations into writing.

Philosophy

Philosophy encompasses a wide range of thought that seeks to explore fundamental questions about existence, morality, knowledge, and the nature of God. The term "philosophy" originates from the Greek word "philo-sophia" which means the love of wisdom. This inquiry is explicitly and intentionally conducted within a Christian framework.
