Practical

1. Patience - A chapter from John Gill's "Body of Practical Divinity" on Christian patience in life.

2. Reformation - Lectures from John Quincy Adams entitled "Baptists, The Only Thorough Religious Reformers," which teach the importance of reformation in the Church and demonstrate the inconsistency of Paedobaptism with New Testament principles.

3. Lectures To My Students - Lectures from Charles Spurgeon to encourage and help those in the Christian ministry with practical and doctrinal wisdom.
