Apologetics to Mormonism

Apologetics to Mormonism involves providing a reasoned defense of the Christian faith in response to the teachings of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS). This field addresses key theological distinctions, such as the nature of God, the role of grace, the authority of Scripture, and the person and work of Jesus Christ. Engaging in apologetics with members of the LDS church requires a deep understanding of both biblical doctrine and the unique teachings of Mormonism.
