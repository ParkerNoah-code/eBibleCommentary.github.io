God

1. The Nature of God - There is one God, the Maker, Preserver and Ruler of all things, having in and of Himself all perfections. To Him all creatures owe the highest love, reverence and obedience.

2. The Trinity - There is one God who eternally exists as three distinct persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit. Each person is fully God, co-equal and co-eternal, sharing the same divine essence, yet they are personally distinct from one another.

3. More than a Calvinist - An essay by John Newton about the importance of living a life, humble and gracious, in keeping with our theology and avoiding the temptations to pride and arrogance that come with knowledge.
