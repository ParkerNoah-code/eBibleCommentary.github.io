Canon

The canon refers to the authoritative body of writings that God has provided to govern the church. Identifying the canon of Scripture is a theological and historical task. We can be confident that God guided the process to preserve and illuminate the canon.

Method

I discuss specific details about canonicity for each book in the Old and New Testament pages. The purpose here is to go over broad historical trends, and to discuss the theological basis for the canon.
