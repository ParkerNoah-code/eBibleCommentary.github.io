Trinity:

Francis Turretin, Institutes

THIRD TOPIC: THE ONE AND TRIUNE GOD

23. What are the meanings of the words “essence,” “substance,” “subsistence,” “person,” “Trinity,” homoousion in this mystery; and may the church properly use them?.

24. Is the mystery of the Trinity a fundamental article of faith? We affirm against the Socinians and Remonstrants.

25. In the one divine essence are there three distinct persons: the Father, Son and Holy Spirit? We affirm against the Socinians.

26. Can the mystery of the Trinity be proved from the Old Testament, and was it known under it? We affirm against the Socinians.

27. Can the divine persons be distinguished from the essence, and from each other, and how?.

28. Is the Son true and eternal God, coessential and coeternal with the Father? We affirm against Socinus.

29. Was the Son of God begotten of the Father from eternity? We affirm.

30. Is the Holy Spirit a divine person, distinct from the Father and the Son? We affirm.

31. Did the Holy Spirit proceed from the Father and the Son? We affirm.
