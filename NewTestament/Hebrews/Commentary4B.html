B. Judgment: Vengeance is Mine (10:26-31)

Ver 26. For if we sin willfully after we have received the knowledge of the truth, there remains no more a sacrifice for sins,

Ver 27. but a certain fearful expectation of judgment, and a fierceness of fire which will devour the adversaries.

Ver 28. A man who disregards Moses’ law dies without compassion on the word of two or three witnesses.

Ver 29. How much worse punishment do you think he will be judged worthy of who has trodden under foot the Son of God, and has counted the blood of the covenant with which he was sanctified an unholy thing, and has insulted the Spirit of grace?

Ver 30. For we know him who said, “Vengeance belongs to me. I will repay,” says the Lord. Again, “The Lord will judge his people.”

Ver 31. It is a fearful thing to fall into the hands of the living God.
