F. Faith: Looking Forward (11:8-12)

Ver 8. By faith Noah, being warned about things not yet seen, moved with godly fear, prepared a ship for the saving of his house, through which he condemned the world and became heir of the righteousness which is according to faith.

Ver 9. By faith he lived as an alien in the land of promise, as in a land not his own, dwelling in tents with Isaac and Jacob, the heirs with him of the same promise.

Ver 10. For he was looking for the city which has foundations, whose builder and maker is God.

Ver 11. By faith even Sarah herself received power to conceive, and she bore a child when she was past age, since she counted him faithful who had promised.

Ver 12. Therefore as many as the stars of the sky in multitude, and as innumerable as the sand which is by the sea shore, were fathered by one man, and him as good as dead.
