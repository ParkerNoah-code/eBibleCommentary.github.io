H. Christian Obedience and Work (3:18-4:1)

Ver 18. Wives, be in subjection to your husbands, as is fitting in the Lord.

Ver 19. Husbands, love your wives, and don’t be bitter against them.

Ver 20. Children, obey your parents in all things, for this pleases the Lord.

Ver 21. Fathers, don’t provoke your children, so that they won’t be discouraged.

Ver 22. Servants, obey in all things those who are your masters according to the flesh, not just when they are looking, as men pleasers, but in singleness of heart, fearing God.

Ver 23-24. And whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men, knowing that from the Lord you will receive the reward of the inheritance; for you serve the Lord Christ.

Ver 25. But he who does wrong will receive again for the wrong that he has done, and there is no partiality.

Ver 1. Masters, give to your servants that which is just and equal, knowing that you also have a Master in heaven.
