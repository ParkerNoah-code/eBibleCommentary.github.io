Colossians Commentary:
I. Opening: Grace and Peace (1:1-2)
II. Thanksgiving: Faith, Prayer, Creation (1:3-23)
III. Body: Christian Maturity (1:24-4:6)
A. Rejoicing amidst Suffering (1:24-2:5)
B. Being Rooted in Christ (2:6-15)
C. Christ is the Center (2:16-19)
D. No Obligation to Teachings of Man (2:20-23)
E. Set your Mind on things Above (3:1-4)
F. Put on the New Self (3:5-13)
G. United Body in Christ (3:14-17)
H. Christian Obedience and Work (3:18-4:1)
I. Praying for the Word to go Out (4:2-6)
IV. Conclusion: Concern and Exhortation (4:7-18)

C. Christ is the Center (2:16-19)

Ver 16-17. Let no one therefore judge you in eating or drinking, or with respect to a feast day or a new moon or a Sabbath day, which are a shadow of the things to come; but the body is Christ’s.

Ver 18. Let no one rob you of your prize by self-abasement and worshiping of the angels, dwelling in the things which he has not seen, vainly puffed up by his fleshly mind,

Ver 19. and not holding firmly to the Head, from whom all the body, being supplied and knit together through the joints and ligaments, grows with God’s growth.
