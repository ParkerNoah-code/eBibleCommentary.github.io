B. Purse a Godly Life Diligently (1:5-7)

Ver 5. Yes, and for this very cause adding on your part all diligence, in your faith supply moral excellence; and in moral excellence, knowledge;

Yes, and for this very cause.

Adding on your part all diligence.

In your faith supply moral excellence.

And in moral excellence, knowledge.

Ver 6. and in knowledge, self-control; and in self-control, perseverance; and in perseverance, godliness;

And in knowledge, self-control.

And in self-control, perseverance.

And in perseverance, godliness.

Ver 7. and in godliness, brotherly affection; and in brotherly affection, love.

And in godliness, brotherly affection.

And in brotherly affection, love.
