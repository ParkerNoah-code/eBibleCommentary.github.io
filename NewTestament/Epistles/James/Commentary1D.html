D. Humbleness: the Poor and the Rich (1:9-11)

Ver 9. Let the brother in humble circumstances glory in his high position;

Ver 10. and the rich, in that he is made humble, because like the flower in the grass, he will pass away.

Ver 11. For the sun arises with the scorching wind and withers the grass; and the flower in it falls, and the beauty of its appearance perishes. So the rich man will also fade away in his pursuits.
