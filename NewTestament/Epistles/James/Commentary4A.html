A. Can that Faith Save Him? (2:14-17)

Ver 14. What good is it, my brothers, if a man says he has faith, but has no works? Can faith save him?

Ver 15-16. And if a brother or sister is naked and in lack of daily food, and one of you tells them, “Go in peace. Be warmed and filled;” yet you didn’t give them the things the body needs, what good is it?

Ver 17. Even so faith, if it has no works, is dead in itself.
