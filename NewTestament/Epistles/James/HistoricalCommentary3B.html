B. The Royal Law of Love (2:8-13)

Ver 8. However, if you fulfill the royal law according to the Scripture, “You shall love your neighbor as yourself,” you do well.

Ver 9. But if you show partiality, you commit sin, being convicted by the law as transgressors.

Ver 10. For whoever keeps the whole law, and yet stumbles in one point, he has become guilty of all.

Ver 11. For he who said, “Do not commit adultery,” also said, “Do not commit murder.” Now if you do not commit adultery but do murder, you have become a transgressor of the law.

Ver 12. So speak and so do as men who are to be judged by the law of freedom.

Ver 13. For judgment is without mercy to him who has shown no mercy. Mercy triumphs over judgment.
