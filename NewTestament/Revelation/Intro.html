Purpose and Message

"This is the Revelation of Jesus Christ, which God gave him to show to his servants the things which must happen soon, which he sent and made known by his angel to his servant, John, who testified to God's word of the testimony of Jesus Christ, about everything that he saw." (Rev. 1:1-2). Revelation is a highly symbolic book consisting of visions and messages that John received and wrote down. It describes the last days, the period between the resurrection and the second coming, and the end of the world which brings with it the ultimate victory of God and the consummated Kingdom of God.
