D. Everything in Subjection to Him (2:5-9)

Ver 5. For he didn’t subject the world to come, of which we speak, to angels.

Ver 6-8a. But one has somewhere testified, saying, “What is man, that you think of him? Or the son of man, that you care for him? You made him a little lower than the angels. You crowned him with glory and honor. You have put all things in subjection under his feet.”

Ver 8b. For in that he subjected all things to him, he left nothing that is not subject to him. But now we don’t yet see all things subjected to him.

Ver 9. For in that he subjected all things to him, he left nothing that is not subject to him. But now we don’t yet see all things subjected to him.
