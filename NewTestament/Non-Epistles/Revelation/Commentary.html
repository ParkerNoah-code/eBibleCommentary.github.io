Revelation
Revelation Text

I. Prologue (1:1-20)
A. Introduction, Purpose, and Blessing (1:1-3)
B. Epistolary Opening (1:4-6)
C. Announcement of the Coming King (1:7-8)
D. John’s Commission (1:9-20)
II. The Seven Churches (2:1-3:22)
A. To Ephesus (2:1-7)
B. To Smyrna (2:8-11)
C. To Pergamum (2:12-17)
D. To Thyatira (2:18-29)
E. To Sardis (3:1-6)
F. To Philadelphia (3:7-13)
G. To Laodicea (3:14-22)
III. Glorified: Christ’s Resurrection (4:1-5:14)
A. Glorified: Judge and Redeemer (4:1-11)
B. Glorified: Death and Resurrection (5:1-14)
IV. Seven Seals (6:1-8:5)
A. Seals 1-6 (6:1-17)
B. Interlude - 144,000 Sealed (7:1-17)
C. Seal 7 (8:1-5)
V. Seven Trumpets (8:6-11:19)
A. The Six Trumpets (8:6-9:21)
B. Interlude - John’s Prophetic Call (10:1-11)
C. Interlude - Witness of the Church (11:1-14)
D. The Seventh Trumpet (11:15-19)
VI. Seven Visions of Deeper Conflict (12:1-15:4)
A. Woman and Seed and the Serpent (12:1-17)
B. Persecution: Beast from the sea (13:1-10)
C. Persecution: Beast from the land (13:11-18)
D. Lamb praised from the 144,000 (14:1-5)
E. Gospel and three Angel Judgment (14:6-13)
F. Son of Man Harvesting of Earth (14:14-20)
G. Victory and Praises Sung to God (15:1-4)
VII. Seven Bowl Judgments (15:5-16:21)
A. Seven Bowl Judgments (15:5-8)
B. The First Six Bowl Judgments (16:1-12)
C. Interlude – Armageddon (16:13-16)
D. Last Bowl Judgment (16:17-21)
VIII. Final Judgment (17:1-19:21)
A. Babylon’s Power and Luxury (17:1-15)
B. Babylon’s Fall (17:16-19:10)
C. Full and Final Destruction (19:11-21)
IX. The Millennium (20:1-15)
A. Inauguration: Millennial Kingdom (20:1-6)
B. Unsuccessful Assault: the Church (20:7-10)
C. Final Judgment (20:11-15)
X. New Heavens and New Earth (21:1-22:5)
A. Dwelling in the Midst of God (21:1-8)
B. New Creation (21:9-22:5)
XI. Epilogue (22:6-21)
