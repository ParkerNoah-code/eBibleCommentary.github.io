Cultural Symbols

Zombies: Zombies are the monster of the day. Zombies represent ugliness, societal breakdown, consumerism, poverty, and sacrilege and are often accompanied with an apocalypse representing environmental degredation and fear of societal collapse. The prevelance of zombies in our culture is a symptom of a larger breakdown in personal, social, political, and religious domains of life.
