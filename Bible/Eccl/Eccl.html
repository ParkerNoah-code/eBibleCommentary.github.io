Articles

Ecclesiastes, SBJT 15.3 (Fall 2011).

Eric Ortlund, The Gospel in the Book of Ecclesiastes, JETS 56/4 (2013), 697-706. Ortlund writes: "Qohelet does not speak about life without God, but of life of both sinner and saint under the sun, and God's strange manner of ordering (or, perhaps better, 'frustrating') our lives." This view seems to align more with the text and consequently with the Gospel than the majority view. He defends this understanding of Ecclesiastes and then explicates it's implications for the Gospel message.
