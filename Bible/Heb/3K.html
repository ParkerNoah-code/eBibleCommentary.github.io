III. Our Great High Priest (7:1-10:18)

K. Christ High and Lifted Up (10:11-18)

Ver 11. Every priest indeed stands day by day serving and offering often the same sacrifices, which can never take away sins,

Ver 12. but he, when he had offered one sacrifice for sins forever, sat down on the right hand of God,

Ver 13. but he, when he had offered one sacrifice for sins forever, sat down on the right hand of God,

Ver 14. but he, when he had offered one sacrifice for sins forever, sat down on the right hand of God,

Ver 15. but he, when he had offered one sacrifice for sins forever, sat down on the right hand of God,

Ver 16a. “This is the covenant that I will make with them after those days,” says the Lord, “I will put my laws on their heart, I will also write them on their mind;”

Ver 16b-17. then he says, 17 “I will remember their sins and their iniquities no more.”

Ver 18. Now where remission of these is, there is no more offering for sin.

James Haldane, Commentary on Hebrews.
