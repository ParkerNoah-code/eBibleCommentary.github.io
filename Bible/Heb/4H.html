IV. Faith: Examples and Exhortation (10:19-12:3)

H. Faith: Passed Down (11:17-22)

Ver 17. By faith, Abraham, being tested, offered up Isaac. Yes, he who had gladly received the promises was offering up his only born son,

Ver 18. to whom it was said, “Your offspring will be accounted as from Isaac,”

Ver 19. concluding that God is able to raise up even from the dead. Figuratively speaking, he also did receive him back from the dead.

Ver 20. By faith Isaac blessed Jacob and Esau, even concerning things to come.

Ver 21. By faith Jacob, when he was dying, blessed each of the sons of Joseph, and worshiped, leaning on the top of his staff.

Ver 22. By faith Joseph, when his end was near, made mention of the departure of the children of Israel, and gave instructions concerning his bones.
