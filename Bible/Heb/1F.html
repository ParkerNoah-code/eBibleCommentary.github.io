I. The Superiority of Christ (1:1-3:6)

F. High Priest and Apostle (3:1-6)

Ver 1. Therefore, holy brothers, partakers of a heavenly calling, consider the Apostle and High Priest of our confession: Jesus

Ver 2. who was faithful to him who appointed him, as also Moses was in all his house.

Ver 3. For he has been counted worthy of more glory than Moses, because he who built the house has more honor than the house.

Ver 4. For every house is built by someone; but he who built all things is God.

Ver 5-6a. Moses indeed was faithful in all his house as a servant, for a testimony of those things which were afterward to be spoken, but Christ is faithful as a Son over his house.

Ver 6b. We are his house, if we hold fast our confidence and the glorying of our hope firm to the end.
