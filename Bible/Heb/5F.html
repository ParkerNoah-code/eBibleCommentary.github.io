V. Concluding Exhortations (12:4-13:25)

F. Sacrifice of Praise (13:15-19)

Ver 15. Through him, then, let’s offer up a sacrifice of praise to God continually, that is, the fruit of lips which proclaim allegiance to his name.

Ver 16. But don’t forget to be doing good and sharing, for with such sacrifices God is well pleased.

Ver 17. Obey your leaders and submit to them, for they watch on behalf of your souls, as those who will give account, that they may do this with joy and not with groaning, for that would be unprofitable for you.

Ver 18. Pray for us, for we are persuaded that we have a good conscience, desiring to live honorably in all things.

Ver 19. I strongly urge you to do this, that I may be restored to you sooner.
