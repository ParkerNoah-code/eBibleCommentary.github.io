V. Concluding Exhortations (12:4-13:25)

C. Unshakeable Kingdom (12:25-29)

Ver 25. to Jesus, the mediator of a new covenant, and to the blood of sprinkling that speaks better than that of Abel.

Ver 26. to Jesus, the mediator of a new covenant, and to the blood of sprinkling that speaks better than that of Abel.

Ver 27. This phrase, “Yet once more” signifies the removing of those things that are shaken, as of things that have been made, that those things which are not shaken may remain.

Ver 28-29. Therefore, receiving a Kingdom that can’t be shaken, let’s have grace, through which we serve God acceptably, with reverence and awe, for our God is a consuming fire.

James Haldane, Commentary on Hebrews.
