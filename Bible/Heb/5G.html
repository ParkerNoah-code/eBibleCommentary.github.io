V. Concluding Exhortations (12:4-13:25)

G. Doxology (13:20-21)

Ver 20. I strongly urge you to do this, that I may be restored to you sooner.

Ver 21. make you complete in every good work to do his will, working in you that which is well pleasing in his sight, through Jesus Christ, to whom be the glory forever and ever. Amen.

James Haldane, Commentary on Hebrews.
