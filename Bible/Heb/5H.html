V. Concluding Exhortations (12:4-13:25)

H. Conclusion (13:22-25)

Ver 22. But I exhort you, brothers, endure the word of exhortation, for I have written to you in few words.

Ver 23. Know that our brother Timothy has been freed, with whom, if he comes shortly, I will see you.

Ver 24. Greet all of your leaders and all the saints. The Italians greet you.

Ver 25. Grace be with you all. Amen.
