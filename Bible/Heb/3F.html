III. Our Great High Priest (7:1-10:18)

F. The Earthly Sanctuary (9:1-10)

Ver 1. who serve a copy and shadow of the heavenly things, even as Moses was warned by God when he was about to make the tabernacle, for he said, “See, you shall make everything according to the pattern that was shown to you on the mountain.”

Ver 2. who serve a copy and shadow of the heavenly things, even as Moses was warned by God when he was about to make the tabernacle, for he said, “See, you shall make everything according to the pattern that was shown to you on the mountain.”

Ver 3. After the second veil was the tabernacle which is called the Holy of Holies,

Ver 4. having a golden altar of incense and the ark of the covenant overlaid on all sides with gold, in which was a golden pot holding the manna, Aaron’s rod that budded, and the tablets of the covenant;

Ver 5. having a golden altar of incense and the ark of the covenant overlaid on all sides with gold, in which was a golden pot holding the manna, Aaron’s rod that budded, and the tablets of the covenant;

Ver 6. Now these things having been thus prepared, the priests go in continually into the first tabernacle, accomplishing the services,

Ver 7. but into the second the high priest alone, once in the year, not without blood, which he offers for himself and for the errors of the people.

Ver 8. The Holy Spirit is indicating this, that the way into the Holy Place wasn’t yet revealed while the first tabernacle was still standing.

Ver 9. The Holy Spirit is indicating this, that the way into the Holy Place wasn’t yet revealed while the first tabernacle was still standing.

Ver 10. being only (with foods and drinks and various washings) fleshly ordinances, imposed until a time of reformation.
