IV. Faith: Examples and Exhortation (10:19-12:3)

D. Faith: Assurance and Conviction (11:1-3)

Ver 1. Now faith is assurance of things hoped for, proof of things not seen.

Ver 2. For by this, the elders obtained approval.

Ver 3. By faith we understand that the universe has been framed by the word of God, so that what is seen has not been made out of things which are visible.

James Haldane, Commentary on Hebrews.
