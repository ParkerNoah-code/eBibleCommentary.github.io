IV. Faith: Examples and Exhortation (10:19-12:3)

G. Faith: Seeking a Homeland (11:13-16)

Ver 13. These all died in faith, not having received the promises, but having seen them and embraced them from afar, and having confessed that they were strangers and pilgrims on the earth.

Ver 14. For those who say such things make it clear that they are seeking a country of their own.

Ver 15. If indeed they had been thinking of that country from which they went out, they would have had enough time to return.

Ver 16. But now they desire a better country, that is, a heavenly one. Therefore God is not ashamed of them, to be called their God, for he has prepared a city for them.

James Haldane, Commentary on Hebrews.
