Exodus

Author

What has been said to the book of Genesis also applies to Exodus. In Genesis Moses depended on the revelations of God and possibly on the accounts of his ancestors while he was mostly able to write down the happenings of Exodus to Deuteronomy by his own view and experience under the guidance of the Holy Spirit (compare Exod 17:14; Deut 31:22, 24).

In the New Testament Moses is mentioned several times as author of Exodus (e.g. Lk 20:37; Mk 7:10).
