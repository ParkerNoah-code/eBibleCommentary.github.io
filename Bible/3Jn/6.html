3 John

VI. Closing (13-15)

13. I had many things to write to you, but I am unwilling to write to you with ink and pen;

Having much more to say, as 2 Jn 12, he resolved on a more immediate, grateful, and effectual way of imparting and even impressing his sense.

14. but I hope to see you soon. Then we will speak face to face.

Speak face to face; viz. by oral conference, which he hoped to have opportunity for.

15. Peace be to you. The friends greet you. Greet the friends by name.

He concludes with the usual Christian salutations.
