Mini Essays

The idea for writing mini essays came from Odysseas' videos Mini Essays: The Ultimate Learning Tool and How to Write a Mini-Essay. Mini essays utilize the principles from the Feynman technique, which is to research, teach, fill in gaps in your understanding, repeat until competent, and then simply. Mini essays do this on a very small scale, are great for memorization, organization, and can be used later on in larger projects, linking multiple ideas together into a chain of reasoning. They typically consist of only 3 to 5 paragraphs.
