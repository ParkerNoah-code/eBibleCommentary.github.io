Foundation of Articulate Speech

Law of Audience

Grasp the background and interests of those you're addressing, and adjust your language and style to fit their needs.

Law of Structure

Organize your thoughts logically with a clear beginning, middle, and end to ensure your message is coherent and impactful.

Enhancing Clarity and Precision

Law of Deliberate Words

Deliberately select each word to ensure your message is clear and impactful.

Law of Precision Pause

Develop the habit of taking a moment to find the most accurate and fitting word for what you want to convey.

Expanding Your Vocabulary

Law of Word Bank

Enhance your ability to speak effortlessly by continually expanding your vocabulary and using new words in context.

Law of Environment

Surround yourself with diverse and sophisticated language settings to naturally enhance your speech.

Depth and Nuance in Speech

Law of Deep Thought

Spend time delving into your ideas to express them with greater depth and clarity.

Law of Humility

Recognize and admit your limitations as a way to foster more genuine and articulate exchanges.

Crafting Eloquent Sentences

Law of Flow

Construct sentences that not only convey your message clearly but also flow smoothly and sound elegant.

Law of Conciseness

Identify and remove unnecessary words to make your speech more powerful and concise.

Mindfulness at Key Moments

Law of Powerful Pause

Develop the habit of taking a moment to find the most accurate and fitting word for what you want to convey.

Law of Mindful Moments

Be especially careful with your word choice at critical junctures, as a few thoughtless words can undermine your entire message.
