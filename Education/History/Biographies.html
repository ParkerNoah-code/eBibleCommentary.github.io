Biographies

Ancient Greece

Pericles (495-429 BC)

A statesman of unparalleled influence, whose leadership not only shaped the course of Athenian history but also left a lasting legacy on Western civilization.

Alexander the Great (356-323 BC)

He was a figure of extraordinary ambition, intelligence, and military skill, whose conquests reshaped the ancient world and left a lasting legacy that continues to influence modern civilization.

Ancient Rome

Julius Caesar (100-44 BC)

The man who crossed the Rubicon, conquered Gaul, and transformed Rome, remains one of history's most iconic figures, a leader whose influence is still felt today.
